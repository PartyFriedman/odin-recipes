Fatboys no spice hot dog

Fat Boy's No-Spice Hot Dog is a flavorful and satisfying option for those who prefer a milder taste without the heat of traditional spicy sausages. Made with high-quality ingredients, this hot dog delivers a rich, savory flavor without any added spices, making it ideal for all ages and palates. Whether served in a soft bun with classic toppings like mustard and ketchup or paired with your favorite sides, Fat Boy's No-Spice Hot Dog is a delicious, comforting choice for casual meals, barbecues, or family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Hot dog bun
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • No spice

Steps

  1. toast the hotdog bun slightly in the oven
  2. Fry the hot dog in a pan
  3. combine the hot dog with the hot dog bun
  4. Add ustard and Ketchup
