Fatboys low-carb lasana

Fatboy's Low-Carb Lasagna is a delicious, healthier alternative to traditional lasagna, designed for those following low-carb or ketogenic diets. Instead of using regular pasta, this lasagna incorporates layers of cheese and low-carb vegetables like zucchini or eggplant, combined with rich meat sauce and creamy cheese blends. It's a hearty, flavorful dish that satisfies cravings without the excess carbs, making it perfect for anyone looking to enjoy comfort food while maintaining a low-carb lifestyle.
