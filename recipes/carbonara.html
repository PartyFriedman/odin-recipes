Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Pasta
  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino romanno
  • Whole peppers

Steps

  1. On low heat, start rendering the diced Guanciale
  2. make sauce by combining egg yolk, pepper an pecorino romano
  3. Add the rendered fat to the sauce
  4. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente
  5. combine pasta and sauce
  6. Add the fried guanciale bits and stir rigourosly
